# Source-of-Truth Policy

Until the missing approved source packages are supplied, the repository's pre-audit documents are planning inputs only. They may not silently become gameplay authority.

Each decision must record: ID, date, owner, authority, affected systems, decision, status, and superseded decision. Draft formulas, lifecycle rules, screen mappings, and configuration values require explicit approval before implementation.

Technical implementation choices may be made without changing gameplay meaning. Product meaning, scope, formulas, lifecycle transitions, time behavior, and financial behavior require product/architecture approval.
